## Daily Predictions

Every day at **midnight EST**, five new predictions are posted about upcoming Trump policy decisions. Each prediction is binary:

- **🌮 TACO** — You believe Trump will reverse course, cave, or walk back the policy
- **💪 HOLDS** — You believe Trump will follow through and execute the policy

<Note>
  Predictions reset daily. You must vote **before 8 PM EST** to be eligible for that day's prizes.
</Note>

---

## How to Vote

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get a Phantom Wallet">
    Download [Phantom](https://phantom.app) — the leading Solana wallet. It's free.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect at TrumpCheck.one">
    Click **Connect Wallet** at the top of the page. Approve the connection in Phantom — no SOL needed to vote.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cast Your Vote">
    For each prediction, tap **TACO** or **HOLDS**. Your vote is recorded instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for 8 PM EST Verdict">
    The verdict cron runs at 8 PM EST every day, evaluating each prediction based on real news sources.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Winning Prizes

The daily prize pool is funded by:
- Platform fees from $TRUMPCHECK token transactions
- Community contributions
- Sponsor allocations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Prize Currency" icon="dollar-sign">
    All prizes are paid in **USDC** — the stablecoin pegged to the US dollar. No volatility risk.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Distribution" icon="scale-balanced">
    The prize pool is split equally among all correct voters for each winning prediction.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Prize Calculation Example

If today's prize pool is **$500 USDC** split across 5 predictions ($100 each), and 50 people correctly voted TACO on one prediction:

```
$100 ÷ 50 = $2.00 USDC per correct voter
```

The more unique your correct prediction (less people voted the same way), the larger your individual share.

## Verdict Rules

Our verdict system uses multiple signals:

| Signal | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Official White House statements | High |
| Reuters / AP news reports | High |
| Congressional Record | Medium |
| Social media (official accounts) | Medium |

<Warning>
  Verdicts are final. The 8 PM EST cron is automated and based on publicly available information at that time.
</Warning>
