## Quick Start

<Steps>

  <Step title="Buy SOL">
    Get SOL from any major exchange:

    | Exchange | Notes |

    | Kraken | Low fees |
    | Binance | Global |
    | Moonpay | Buy directly in Phantom |

    Transfer SOL to your Phantom wallet address. Start with $50–$100 worth for trading + fees.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Buy on Pump.fun (Easiest)">
    1. Go to [Pump.fun](https://pump.fun)
    2. Search for **TRUMPCHECK**
    3. Connect your Phantom wallet
    4. Enter the amount of SOL you want to swap
    5. Click **Buy** and confirm in Phantom
  </Step>

  <Step title="Trade on Raydium (More Liquidity)">
    1. Go to [Raydium.io](https://raydium.io/swap/)
    2. Connect Phantom wallet
    3. Paste the **$TRUMPCHECK contract address** (from TrumpCheck.one)
    4. Set SOL as the input token and TRUMPCHECK as the output
    5. Approve the swap

    <Tip>
      Set slippage to 5–10% for best results, especially for larger trades.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Vote & Win">

    1. Go to [TrumpCheck.one](https://trumpcheck.one)
    2. Connect your Phantom wallet
    3. Vote on today's 5 predictions
    4. Check back at 8 PM EST for verdicts and prizes
  </Step>
</Steps>
