## Token Utility

### Prediction Market Fees
A small $TRUMPCHECK fee is collected on prediction market entries above a threshold. This funds the USDC prize pool.

### Prize Distributions
Daily prediction winners receive $TRUMPCHECK alongside USDC prizes (bonus rewards).

### Governance (Planned)
Future community votes on prediction categories, charity allocations, and platform features will be weighted by $TRUMPCHECK holdings.
